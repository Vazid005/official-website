Streak 1v1 Season 1


1v1 Solo Mid Tournament



INFORMATION AND RULES

Moderator may over rule all rules based on the situation. Moderator's decision is final after the appeal process.Each person attending the tournament must have read and accepted the rules and regulations. Ignoring it cannot be a reason put forward in case of an issue or dispute.

REGISTRATION PROCESS:

  • Players are required to get themselves registered for the tournament before the end date.
  • The registration form is available on the particular site page.
  • There are no standins allowed.
  • Remember it is a free to join tournament so there will be lots of players and lots of shit to deal with, that is why we will strictly implement the rules. We donâ€™t bother if you have emergency at exact time or whatever. When it is time for match, you will have to be here.
LOBBY SETTINGS:

  • Region will be Singapore or India according to players choice. EU can also be requested.
  • Game Mode: 1v1 Solo Mid
  • Enable Cheats: OFF
  • Bot: OFF
  • Version: Tournament
  • Series Type: Bo1; Finals: Bo3
  • Starting Team: Random
  • Spectators: ENABLE
  • Dota TV Delay: 5 minutes
HOW MATCHES WILL BE PLAYED:

  • Match Schedule including date and time will be shown on our matches page.
  • Match lobby will be manually hosted by Moderators and Password will be given to players before 10 minutes by email.
  • Both the players should join and then only the match begins.
  • In case of Best of 3 and 5, all matches will be go consecutively and cannot be postponed to another day.
  • Teams must be ready atleast 10 minutes before match time.
  • In case any player is not ready after 10 minutes of match time, then that team will be declared loser and will be kicked from tournament automatically.
  • If there is a technical problem that requires a rematch, contact your repective moderator.
  • Incase of a rematch, a match has to be played with the same picks.
  • Any actions that result in an unfair advantage are illegal. This includes bug abuse of any kind.
  • Spectators inside the Lobby are forbidden
  • Casting on the match is only allowed with an admin agreement. To get an agreement please kindly Contact Us.
  • Soul Ring and Dagon are disallowed in the game.

WINNING CRITERIA:

  • If a player forfeits the match and abandons or rage quits
  • If a player does not show up on time and you are given a walk over by Moderator.
  • If a player is disqualified for any reason, opponent will be declared winner.
  • Once a player's T1 Tower is destroyed or is killed twice by the opponent, opponent will be winner of the match.
  • Once a player dies, he has to type in GG and the opponent will be winner of the match.
  • If your opponent calls GG, wait for their throne to be destroyed. (In a tournament once an enemy team calls GG, system automatically ask the GG calling team if they want to forfeit this match. Once they accept that option, their throne is destroyed automatically)
PRIZES:

  • The 1st place holder of the tournament will be awarded with an Immortal Bundle.
  • The 2nd place holder of the tournament will be awarded with an Mythical Bundle.
  • The 3rd place holder of the tournament will be awarded with an Rare Bundle.
  • The 4th place holder of the tournament will be awarded with an Common Bundle.
  • The prizes will be sent to the respective people by admin using the in-game option 'Gift'or 'Trade'
