Streak 1v1 Season 1
  1. 1v1 Solo Mid Tournament
See Schedule & Brackets

Streak 1v1 Season 1 is a Solo Mid Dota 2 tournament. Participants are required to read the rules before registering. This is a free tier tournament, so registrations are free. Registrants can play at their own comfort zone as this is an online event.


Prizes


1st Place - Immortal Bundle
2nd Place - Mythical Bundle
3rd Place - Rare Bundle
4th Place - Comman Bundle

Settings


These will be the lobby settings for the tournament.

  • Region will be India by default. If both players agree with a different server option, then the server will be that. EU can also be requested.
  • Game Mode: 1v1 Solo Mid
  • Enable Cheats: OFF
  • Bot: OFF
  • Version: Tournament
  • Series Type: Bo1; Finals: Bo3
  • Starting Team: Random
  • Spectators: ENABLE
  • Dota TV Delay: 5 minutes
